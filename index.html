How It Works

A multi-layered approach to ensure comprehensive safety.

Automated PPE Detection

Our AI model scans live video feeds to ensure every worker is equipped with the necessary Personal Protective Equipment, from helmets to high-visibility vests, reducing compliance risks automatically.

Proactive Ergonomics Analysis

By analyzing body posture and movement, the system identifies potentially harmful actions or proximity to machinery, helping to prevent musculoskeletal injuries and other physical risks.
